Automated Fare Collection and Dynamic Stop Notification System for Time Efficient Bus Operations

This patent document presents a novel system for automated fare collection and passenger management in public transportation, designed to optimize efficiency and enhance passenger experience. The system integrates various components to streamline fare payment processes and improve operational workflows. Upon boarding, passengers tap their smart cards to a module that records their boarding position and other vital information. This data is simultaneously kept in local and global databases. The local database updates the driver's display in real time with passenger counts, available seats, and forthcoming bus stops that require passengers to alight, allowing for more informed decision-making. In addition, the system prioritizes accessibility by allowing passengers with disabilities to pre-book, with drivers receiving early notice. Passengers swipe their cards once again for fare deduction while deboarding at their destination. If a card is not tapped during the deboarding process, there will be a penalty that will be taken out of the card during the next trip.To ensure fare compliance in the event of inadequate capital, fares are subtracted from the minimum deposit made for the card. GPS units also monitor bus movements, which improves operational supervision and route optimisation. This integrated system is expected to optimize bus operations in urban transportation networks, boost accessibility, and streamline passenger management.
